# The Counter Earth — Visual Style Guide

## Brand Identity

Create all assets in a polished stylised survival-adventure game style suitable for Roblox. The world should feel cohesive across two halves of the experience: ruined infected Earth and the hopeful Counter Earth. Earth should feel weathered, dangerous, abandoned, and post-apocalyptic. The Counter Earth should feel fresh, pastoral, low-tech, handmade, and full of quiet hope, with a simple medieval survival feel.

## 3D Models

All 3D models should be low-poly, clean, readable, and game-ready, with strong silhouettes and simple stylised forms. Prioritise clear shapes over fine detail. Avoid realism, noisy topology, fragile tiny elements, and overbuilt geometry. Assets should read well at gameplay distance and in inventory icons.

## Textures & Materials

Textures should be stylised, matte, and readable, with visible material definition but no photorealism. Use soft colour breakup, simple surface character, and low-frequency detail. Avoid realistic dirt noise, harsh grunge, glossy materials, micro-detail, and overly complex patterns. Materials should feel clean, handcrafted, and slightly exaggerated for readability.

## Characters

Characters should use a soft rounded 3D chibi style with large smooth heads, small compact bodies, short limbs, simple expressive faces, and polished stylised proportions. They should not look blocky, realistic, anime, tactical, or military. Hair, clothing, and gear should feel readable, slightly simplified, and consistent with a stylised survival world.

## UI, Icons & Promotional Art

UI, icons, logos, and promotional art should match the same brand language: polished, readable, adventurous, slightly post-apocalyptic but hopeful, with a fun 13+ survival tone. The whole project should feel atmospheric, game-like, and inviting rather than grim, realistic, or horror-heavy.

## Keywords (Include in Prompts)

stylised, low-poly, matte, readable, chibi, polished, survival-adventure, handcrafted, weathered but hopeful, Roblox-friendly, atmospheric, clean shapes, soft cinematic lighting, game-ready, cohesive worldbuilding.

## Avoid (Exclude from Prompts)

photorealism, military shooter style, tactical realism, grimdark horror, glossy surfaces, noisy textures, micro-detail, realistic anatomy, anime proportions, blocky toy-like forms, cluttered silhouettes, overcomplicated assets.
